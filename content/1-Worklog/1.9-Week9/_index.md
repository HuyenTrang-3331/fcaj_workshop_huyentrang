### Week 9 Achievements:

- Reviewed foundational knowledge of AWS Cloud.
- Systematized the role of main service groups in AWS, including:
  - Compute.
  - Storage.
  - Networking.
  - Database.
  - Security.
  - Management Tools.
- Deepened understanding of IAM's role in identity management and access control on AWS.
- Mastered key components in IAM, including:
  - User.
  - Group.
  - Role.
  - Policy.
  - Permission.
- Understood the Least Privilege principle and why to grant the minimum necessary permissions to users or services.
- Recognized the importance of MFA in protecting AWS accounts.
- Understood the role of IAM Identity Center in centralized login management.
- Learned how to grant Billing-related permissions to IAM Users.
- Mastered some common causes of costs on AWS, including:
  - EC2 Instances running continuously.
  - RDS Database Instances not stopped or deleted.
  - NAT Gateway incurring hourly and data processing costs.
  - CloudWatch Logs storing too many logs.
  - Load Balancers no longer used but not deleted.
- Learned how to use AWS Budget and Cost Explorer to track costs.
- Developed the habit of checking resources after completing lab exercises.
- Learned how to clean up unused resources to avoid unnecessary costs.
- Completed study notes on IAM, account security, and cost optimization.
